## Changes Made

Three changes were identified by diffing the manually edited lessons against the
baseline revision:

1. **Exercise headings**: Removed "Exercise A:" and "Exercise B:" prefixes, leaving
   just the exercise name and time (e.g., `### Career Conversation Practice (12 min)`).

2. **Em-dashes**: Replaced `---` with `—` throughout prose, per the style guide.

3. **Prose reformatting**: Broke long prose paragraphs into shorter wrapped lines
   (one sentence or clause per line), matching the style of performance, governance,
   lifecycle, safety, and project lesson files.
